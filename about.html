ABOUT

Milind Palav entered the world of advertising in 1995,
with a degree from the prestigious Sir. J.J. Institute of Applied Art.



He worked with top advertising agencies from India and overseas including Enterprise, Scanad LOWE Nairobi, EURO RSCG Mumbai, SSC&B LINTAS Mumbai, Saatchi & Saatchi Bangalore, Lintas Mumbai, DDB Mudra Mumbai, McCann Mumbai. His last stint was with FCB Interface, Mumbai as a senior creative director.



Over two decades, Milind has played a key role in building a wide array of brands across sectors, including Bajaj Electricals, Reliance Mobile, Loop Mobile, Phillips, Volkswagen, Union Bank of India, Coca Cola, Nirav Modi, IFB, Hyundai, Mahindra and Mahindra, Simply Marry, Act II, Perfetti, Hotel Renaissance, Kenya Airways, and Jet Airways.



His work has been recognized both locally and globally, having earned mentions at D&AD, Cannes, Ad Fest, One Show, Clio, Loerie (South Africa), New York Festival and AAAI.



Currently, a digital design & execution freelancer,
open for local as well as overseas RETOUCHING & CGI projects.
